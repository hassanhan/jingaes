Whats the tiniest animal in the world?

Kitti’s hog-nosed bat (Craseonycteris thonglongyai), aka the bumbelbee bat,
is the smallest mammal in the world Averaging around 30 millimeters in length,
Kitti’s hog-nosed bat (also known as the bumblebee bat) is officially the world’s smallest mammal – the same size as a bumblebee.
