Who is the King of the jungle?

king of the jungle The lion, especially in cultural or artistic depictions. (Usually a misnomer, as lions typically
inhabit deserts or dry forests, as opposed to jungles.) Engraved in striking marble, the king of the jungle stands sentinel over this ancient arena.
